The Music & The People

The Start

Speck’s Records & Tapes is a family-run, neighborhood record shop. These days, it seems that there are infinite ways to seek out and educate oneself in music. At Speck’s, we believe in the power and personal touch of the local record shop — a place where the conversation never stops, where unearthing new gems is possible, where familiar faces navigate the dense musical seas alongside you.

The Passion

Speck’s is a place to discover, rediscover, and absorb music. As a family, our lives have been very much shaped and defined by the ways we shared, created, and surrounded ourselves with music. We want to extend that same passion and enthusiasm to our customers, as well as learn from them.
